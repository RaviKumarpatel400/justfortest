Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about KaamWalah services

How does KaamWalah work?

KaamWalah connects you with skilled professionals in your area. Simply download our app, search for the service you need, browse through verified professionals, and book your service in just 60 seconds.

How are service providers verified?

All service providers on KaamWalah undergo a thorough verification process including identity verification, skill assessment, and background checks to ensure you receive quality service from trusted professionals.

What areas do you currently serve?

We currently serve Bihar, India, with plans to expand to more regions soon. Our mission is to empower local communities by creating a trusted marketplace for skilled professionals across all trades.

How do I become a service provider on KaamWalah?

To become a service provider, click on "Become a Pro" on our website or app. Complete the registration process, submit required documents for verification, and once approved, you can start receiving service requests.

Is there a guarantee on services?

Yes, we stand behind the quality of our service providers. If you're not satisfied with the service, you can report issues through our app and we'll work to resolve them promptly.
